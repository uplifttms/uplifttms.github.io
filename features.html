Uplift Transport Management System Features


Organisation


  • Enter client details. Create accounts for clients to login and view their orders and deliveries.
  • Define pickup and delivery locations. Select via a map every each places latitude and longitude, allowing for geographic visibility.
  • Enter the details of your own vehicles. Connect us with your GPS provider to see vehicles moving consignments in real time.






Finance


  • First create rate-zones, geographic boundaries which encompass pickup and delivery locations.
  • Next, define company wide or client specific rates. Rates require a 'to' and 'from' rate-zone, along with a rate code (what you are actually carrying). Rates apply to each job, and can be either per unit (quantity, weight, or cubic), or stepped rates.
  • Finally, once a job is created a rate is calculated as assigned and an invoice is created. Once you have approved and verified each invoice, you can batch any number of invoice and send them to your account package of choice.
  • Can generate Xero invoice files, with other accounting package formats on their way.




Dispatch


  • Uplift allows you to book either freight or container jobs. Provide information such as goods information, required dates and time, and special instructions for the drivers.
  • Plan the job's journey using our legging based dispatch tool. Allocate jobs to vehicles and trailers, and define a route for multi-leg jobs. Once happy with a plan, mark it as ready to send to your drivers mobile app.
  • Create lists of jobs with our manifest tool. This allows superior visibility for a group of related jobs






Driver Mobile App


  • Allows drivers to sign in from any mobile device with an internet connection. Limit driver to a single vehicle, or allow jumping between any vehicle they may possibly drive.
  • Gives drivers a list of current jobs. Capture details in real time as drivers use the app to notify dispatch when jobs are uplifted, offloaded, or transferred to another vehicle.
  • Capture digital signatures from recipients with our sign-on-glass feature.
  • Currently available for Android. iOS currently under development.
